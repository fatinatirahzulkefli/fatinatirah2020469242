Short experience

Since school days, I have been active in holding club positions and participating in various activities organized by the school. Among the programs I have participated in a leadership camps, STEM week exhibitions, budding scientists projects, user interactive challenges, pocket talk and many more. Apart from that, I am also active in sports activities organized by the school such as marching events, running , jumping, athletics, cross-country and I have also held positions such as school supervisor, assistant head of class, supreme council of clubs, as well as members of the club's authority. Next, when I entered the university world, I participated in a poster drawing competition organized by the UiTM Dungun peer club, a competition organized by UNAIR under the program of the 2nd National Vocational Conference, KASUKA, the International Student Symposium program, the Youth Survivals Program, and many more. In addition, I also hold a position as the highest council in the INFORMA academic club. Here I have learned various things such as how to manage time well, the procedures required before doing a program, a great sense of responsibility and trust and being able to add experience and knowledge. in addition, I also do a part time job when I have free time. This taught me a lot to be disciplined and responsible for the tasks that I need to complete. I hope to be able to continue to gain new knowledge a nd experience in the future.
